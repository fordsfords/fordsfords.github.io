Tip and Ring

The name for the two wires on a telephone line, derived from the electrical contacts on old-style telephone plugs (which are similar to 1/4-inch headphone plugs). One electrical contact is at the tip of the plug, and the other is a ring just above it (and there is a sleeve above that), as shown in the accompanying figure.
